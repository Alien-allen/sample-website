Steps to cook scrambled eggs.

  • Scranble 3 to 4 eggs in a bowl
  • Add a half teaspoon of salt in the eggs.
  • Preheat a pan and spray some oils in the pan.
  • Pour all the eggs into the pan.
  • Turn down the fire to medium.
  • Stir the eggs softly and slowly for 1 to 2 mins.
  • Add some pepper and salt.
